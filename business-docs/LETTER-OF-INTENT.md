## BETA PROGRAM ACKNOWLEDGMENTS

By signing this Letter of Intent, the undersigned acknowledges and agrees to the following:

**1. Evaluation Purpose**
The organization named above intends to evaluate Ledgr as an accounting and bookkeeping tool during the beta program period. Participation is voluntary and does not constitute a commitment to adopt Ledgr as a permanent software solution.

**2. Free Beta Access**
Ledgr will provide the signing organization with free access to its full platform — including all AI features — during the beta program period. Beta access will not be revoked for good-faith participation regardless of whether the organization ultimately becomes a paying customer.

**3. Feedback Participation**
The signing organization agrees to provide good-faith feedback to Ledgr regarding their experience with the platform, including but not limited to: usability issues, missing features, AI accuracy observations, and comparison to their current workflow. Feedback may be provided via in-app survey, email, or scheduled call. This obligation is not legally enforceable; it is a statement of intent that Ledgr uses to prioritize beta invitations.

**4. Non-Binding Nature**
This document is expressly non-binding. It does not create any obligation on either party to enter into a commercial relationship, purchase agreement, or service contract. Either party may discontinue participation in the beta program at any time without consequence.

**5. Software Disclaimer**
Ledgr is accounting software — it is not an accounting firm, CPA practice, tax advisor, or financial advisor. Ledgr does not provide tax advice, legal advice, or financial advice. Users are responsible for the accuracy of their own books and should consult a licensed CPA or tax professional for tax preparation, audit, and compliance matters. The AI features in Ledgr are designed to assist with bookkeeping tasks and should be reviewed by the user; they do not substitute for professional accounting judgment.
