## SECTION 10: FAQ

**Section heading:** Common questions, honest answers.

**Pre-heading tag:** FAQ

---

**Q1: Is the free tier actually free? What's the catch?**

There is no catch. The free tier is permanently free — not a 14-day trial, not a crippled demo. You get full AI features, full financial reporting, bank reconciliation, and invoice sending. The upgrade to Pro is available when you need more than 5 active clients or want a custom invoice domain. We don't lock core accounting functionality behind a paywall.

---

**Q2: Can I really import my QuickBooks data?**

Yes. Ledgr supports full import of QuickBooks data: transaction history, chart of accounts, client list, and invoice history. The import process takes approximately 20 minutes for most businesses. We have a step-by-step guide, and our support team is available if anything goes wrong.

---

**Q3: Is my financial data secure?**

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption. Bank connections are read-only — Ledgr can view your transactions but cannot move money. We do not sell or share your financial data with third parties. SOC 2 Type II compliance is on our roadmap for Q3 2026.

---

**Q4: What AI model powers Ledgr?**

Ledgr uses OpenAI's GPT-4o model for its AI features. All AI processing uses your data only for the purpose of answering your queries — we do not use your financial data to train general AI models.

---

**Q5: Does Ledgr handle payroll?**

Not yet. Payroll is on our roadmap for Q3 2026. In the meantime, Ledgr integrates with Gusto and ADP for payroll, and syncs payroll journal entries automatically to keep your books accurate.

---

**Q6: Can I use Ledgr if I have an accountant?**

Yes — Ledgr is designed to work with your accountant, not replace them. You can grant your accountant read or edit access to your books. We export to formats your accountant's tools accept. The AI features handle the routine; your accountant handles strategy and compliance.

---

**Q7: What if I need features that aren't available yet?**

Tell us. We have a public roadmap and a direct line to the product team for all paying users. We prioritize features based on what our users actually need, not what's easiest to build. If you need something specific, email founders@ledgr.com.

---

**Q8: What happens to my data if I cancel?**

If you cancel, your data is yours. You can export your full transaction history, reports, and client data in CSV format at any time. We retain your data for 90 days after cancellation in case you want to return. After 90 days, it is permanently deleted per our data retention policy.
